Plamo

I am proud to introduce Plamo, a digital solution for the Plantijn Moretus Museum in Antwerp Belgium.

My mission was to enhance the museum experience and encourage interaction. My app offers engaging mini-games and riddles that challenge visitors to put together a personal book. This creates an interactive, memorable experience that stays with visitors. In addition, they can immortalize their museum visit by adding photos to their book. Plamo also broadens its target audience, allowing families to enjoy the app during their visit.
